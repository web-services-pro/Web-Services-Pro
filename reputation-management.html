Why Regular Reviews Are Failing Your Local SEO

Google has evolved. With features like "Ask Maps" and AI Overviews (AIO), basic phrases like "they did a great job" don't rank your business anymore. AI search engines scan customer reviews for specific details before recommending a contractor.

The Old Way (Fails AI Search)

"John was nice, great job!" — Provides zero context for search bots.

Our AI-Guided Method (Dominates Search)
